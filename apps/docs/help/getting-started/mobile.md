## Current Options

While the native iOS and Android apps are being developed, you can use Notesaner on mobile via:

### Progressive Web App (PWA)

1. Open your Notesaner URL in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
2. Tap the share icon → **Add to Home Screen**
3. The app will open in a full-screen mode with offline support

### Offline Support

The PWA caches recently accessed notes for offline reading. Changes made offline are synced when connectivity is restored using the Yjs CRDT conflict resolution mechanism.
