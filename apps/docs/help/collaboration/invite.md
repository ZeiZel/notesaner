## Invite by Email

1. Go to **Workspace Settings → Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address and select a role:
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access
   - **Editor**: Can create and edit notes
   - **Admin**: Full access including settings and member management

## Share Links

Generate a shareable invite link from **Workspace Settings → Members → Invite Link**. Anyone with the link can join with the default role you set.
