The Slides plugin renders a note as a Reveal.js presentation.

## Slide Separators

Horizontal slides are separated by `---`:

```markdown
# Slide 1

Content of slide 1

---

# Slide 2

Content of slide 2
```

Vertical slides are separated by `--`:

```markdown
# Main Slide

---

## Sub-slide 1

--

## Sub-slide 2
```

## Presenting

Click the **Present** button in the note toolbar to enter fullscreen presentation mode.
