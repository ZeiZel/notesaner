# Login & Access Issues

## Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (check spam if not received)

## SAML / SSO Issues

If your organization uses SSO and you can't log in:

- Contact your workspace admin to verify SSO configuration
- Check that your email matches the SSO provider's user record

## Account Locked

Accounts are temporarily locked after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes before trying again, or use **Forgot Password** to reset.

## Two-Factor Auth Recovery

If you lost access to your authenticator app:

1. On the 2FA screen, click **Use a recovery code**
2. Enter one of your saved recovery codes

If you don't have recovery codes, contact [support](/help/troubleshooting/contact-support).
