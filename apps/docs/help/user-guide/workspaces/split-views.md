## Splitting a Panel

Right-click a tab → **Split Right** or **Split Down** to open the note in a side-by-side view.

## Resizing Panels

Drag the divider between panels to resize them.

## Closing a Split

Click the `×` button in the panel header, or drag a tab to another panel to merge them.
